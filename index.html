About me

Hello, I'm Cintia and I live in the Bay Area in California. I work as a Front End Software Engineer at Yahoo! and I enjoy messing around with fun web stuff.

I recently got my Master's degree at Carnegie Mellon University as a Master of Entertainment Technology, which is most awesome degree to have (in my opinion). I have a reasonable knowledge in HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript, which allows me to make The Web a nicer place to be.

I am very interested in everything that comes out for web development and I am always trying new stuff, specially if it promises to end up in a very fun result - it doesn't matter how hard its path might be to get to the final awesomeness.

I don't consider myself a designer, but I am always picking some of my designer friends' brains to create new experiences and for play testing my most recent "experiment".
